CARBON DIRECT
What is it ?

Carbon Direct is a purpose-driven carbon management firm dedicated to making carbon science actionable with their end-to-end platform. They Enable organizations to reduce, remove, and utilize their emissions with carbon science

What it does
Measure

Understands company's carbon footprint and prioritize actions to reduce and remove emissions.

Remove

Companies purchase high-quality carbon removal supply vetted by Carbon Direct's expert team.

Track & Report

Share company's impact and make data-driven decisions with user-friendly dashboards.
